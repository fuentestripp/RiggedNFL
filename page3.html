Why it Matters

It doesn't

Professional Sports at its core is entertainment. We as consumers care more about having something fun to watch than having something fair and potentially boring.

We as a society have a tendency to take things too seriously, and in the end it really doesn't matter who wins or who loses. Even if the NFL attempts to fix a nationwide narrative, it doesn't really matter. Most people who already care about the sport of football are aware and have accepted the reality. They know what's real and what isn't.
